INGREDIENTS

200,g,full-grain pasta e.g. spirelli
200,g,kale
100,g,sunflower seeds
100,g,grana padano
2,tbsp,lemon juice
200,g,olive oil
2,tsp,Sonnentor Rudi Ruhe Gewuerzmischung
1,pinch,salt
1,pinch,pepper
2,pcs,mid-sized fennel

INSTRUCTIONS

1. Cut cale into stripes, blanche and put it in a blender.
2. Roast sunflower seeds on medium heat until golden brown and add to blender
3. To blender, add lemon juice, olive oil, spice mixture, salt, pepper and mix until it's got a pesto texture.
4. Clean fennel, cut in half and remove stemm. Cut into 1/8th long stripes, roast in pan until golden brown.
5. Cook noodles until al dente.
6, Mix noodles with pesto and garnish with fennel and grana padano. Enjoy.
