INGREDIENTS

100,ml,water
150,ml,orange juice
7,tbsp,oat flakes
2,pcs,carrot
1,tbsp,Sonnentor Wieder gut Irma Immun Gewuerzmischung
1,tsp,cut coriander green

INSTRUCTIONS

1. Finely grate carrots
2. Cook grated carrots with water and orange juice in a pot.
3. Add spice mixture and oats, let it simmer until the porridge has thickened.
4. Garnish with coriander green.
