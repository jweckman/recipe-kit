INGREDIENTS

110,ml,coconut oil
250,ml,maplesyrup
2.5,tbsp,cashew cream
70,g,dried figs
60,g,dates
25,g,sunflower seeds
25,g,sesame
70,g,amaranth
105,g,coconut flakes

INSTRUCTIONS

1. Combine ingredients
2. Spread even layer on a baking tin, press tight with spoon
3. Bake in the oven at 180 degrees for 30min
