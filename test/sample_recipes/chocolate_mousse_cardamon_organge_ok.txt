INGREDIENTS

2,pcs,orange
2,pcs,avocados
2,tbsp,honey
1,tbsp,cacao
1,tsp,orange zest
2,tbsp,almond butter
2,tsp,cardamon latte
10,g,mint

INSTRUCTIONS

1. Juice oranges.
2. Add orange juice, avocados, honey, cacoa, caradmom latte powder, orange zest and almond butter to a mixer and turn into a creamy mixture.
3. Fill 2 glasses with the mixture, decorate with mint and orange pieces.
