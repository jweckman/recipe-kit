INGREDIENTS

6,pcs,banana
3,tbsp,maple syrup
1,tsp,kurkuma latte powder
1,tsp,cardamon latte powder
1,tsp,beetroot latte powder
1,pcs,fresh fruit[optional]
1,pcs,rosted coconut flakes[optional]

INSTRUCTIONS

1. Peel bananas, cut into pieces and put to freezer for 3 hours
2. Put bananas and maple syrup into mixer and mix until smooth
3. Spread cream into 3 bowls, add latte powder to each bowl. Enjoy.
